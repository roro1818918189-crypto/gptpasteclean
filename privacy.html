Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

GPTPasteClean is a client-side tool. All text processing happens locally in your browser.

What data we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any text you paste into the tool. Your content never leaves your device.

Cookies and tracking

GPTPasteClean does not use cookies, analytics, or third-party trackers.

Local storage

The tool may use your browser’s local storage to remember usage limits or interface preferences. This data stays on your device and is never shared.

Third-party services

GPTPasteClean does not integrate with external services or APIs.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
